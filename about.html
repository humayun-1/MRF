The hospital is expanding, Help us to grow so we can so we cater to more needy people.

Mukhtaran Rafiq Foundation was set up in May 2004 in Canal Bank Scheme, Fatehgarh Aziz Bhatti Town, in the shape of a small dispensary to provide free medical treatment to the needy people of the surrounding catchment area against the lack of free medical healthcare in the local area. But the increasing number of patients felt the need to extend its services to 10 bedded hospitals. Our aim is to improve the availability of healthcare to the poor and needy, especially those who are from a non-privileged background. By the grace of ALLAH Almighty after 17 years of continuous efforts, it has become a spectacular hospital.

  • In 2012 Mukhtaran Rafiq Foundation purchased a plot which was endowed to the hospital. The hospital also was registered with the Social Welfare Department and achieved the Tax Exemption Certificate.
  • In 2014, the hospital extended to a 20 bedded hospital along with the facility of Operation Theatre and Labor room.
  • In 2017 the hospital was awarded the Best Achievement Award by the Social Welfare Department.
  • Today Mukhtaran Rafiq Welfare Hospital is serving 350-400 patients daily, out of which approx. 50 patients are served on free bases.
  • The remaining patients are charged Rs. 50 (from 8am to 8pm) and Rs.100 (from 8pm to 8am) for medical checkup and medicines, whereas the average expense per patient is Rs. 350.
  • The average patients for the hospital is 150,000 patients / year, out of which 15,000 patients are treated free of cost Checkup, Medicine and Vitals.
  • The Hospital present history is a four storey building, It has 30 beds capacity. Presently two general wards, two State of the art Operation theater s and five patient rooms.
  • The Hospital is located in a four-story building. It has a 30 bed capacity. Currently with two general wards, two State of the art Operation theater s, and five patient rooms.
  • It has all the necessary medical departments such as OPD/Emergency 24/7, State of the art Operation theater , Orthopaedic Surgery,
  • General Surgery, Gynaecology, Infertility, Dentistry, Sugar, Family Medicine, Diagnostic Laboratory, Radiology, X-ray,Physiotherapy, Hijama, Nutrition, E.N.T, Pulmonology, Dermatology, Psychiatry, Urology, Gerontology.
  • We are also providing free of cost Dialysis services to all patients.

Mukhtaran Rafiq Foundation (MRF) is an independent, non-political, non-profitable and non-governmental social welfare voluntary organization, Registered from Punjab Healthcare Commission, FBR and Pakistan Charity Commission having vision of providing free healthcare services, who cannot afford.
